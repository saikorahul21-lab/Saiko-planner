Golden Rules
Non-negotiable — follow these every single post
01
Har episode ek cliffhanger pe khatam ho
"Agle episode mein batayenge kya hua" — audience ko reason do wapas aane ka. Without this, series nahi chalegi. Ending = hook for next episode.
02
Hook pehle 3 seconds mein
Mistake, surprise, ya question — scroll rokne wala kuch. "Hamara naam almost UNIGLO tha" beats "Aaj mein bataunga branding ke baare mein" every time.
03
Har episode mein ek actionable lesson
Viewer sirf story nahi — kuch seekhna chahta hai. "Trademark pehle" jaise takeaways save aur share hote hain. Yeh aapki reach organically badhata hai.
04
Real visuals only — no stock footage
Actual WhatsApp screenshots, real logos, factory photos, notebook pages. Authenticity hi aapka biggest edge hai over polished brands with big budgets.
05
Caption mein episode number daalo
"Saiko Se Pehle — EP 03" — audience series track karti hai, profile pe aati hai baaki episodes ke liye. Yeh aapka retention mechanism hai.
06
Sunday batch shoot karo
Poore week ke 4–5 reels ek din mein. Outfit change karo episodes ke liye. Same location chalega. Yeh consistency ensure karta hai when launch pressure hits.
07
Subtitles — mandatory, no exceptions
70% log bina sound ke dekhte hain Instagram pe. CapCut auto-subtitle — 2 minutes. Without subtitles, 70% audience miss ho rahi hai. Non-negotiable.
